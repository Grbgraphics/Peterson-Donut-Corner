About Us

We are Petersons Donuts Coners, a family-owned business that has been serving freshly made, hand-crafted donuts for over 30 years. Our love for donuts started as a hobby, and now we have made it our mission to provide the best donuts to our customers.

Our classic glazed donuts are a customer favorite, but we also offer a variety of filled pastries and specialty creations. We believe that the secret to making great donuts is using only the finest ingredients and making everything from scratch.

Thank you for choosing Petersons Donuts Coners, we look forward to serving you!
